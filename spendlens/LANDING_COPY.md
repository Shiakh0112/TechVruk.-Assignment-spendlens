## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. The audit is completely free. No credit card, no login, no trial. We make money when we help companies with larger savings implement the recommendations — that's a separate, optional service.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: We verify all pricing against official vendor pages weekly. Every number in our audit engine cites a source URL. Prices change — we update within 7 days of any vendor pricing change.

**Q: Do you store my data?**
A: Your audit results are stored so you can share the link. Your email (if you provide it) is stored separately and never included in the public shareable URL. We don't sell your data.

**Q: What if I'm already on the optimal plan?**
A: We'll tell you that honestly. "You're spending well" is a valid audit result. We don't manufacture savings. We'll offer to notify you when new optimizations apply to your stack.

**Q: How is this different from just reading the pricing pages myself?**
A: Three ways: (1) We check your specific team size against plan thresholds — most people don't realize Team plans have minimum seat requirements. (2) We surface cross-tool redundancies — paying for both GitHub Copilot and Cursor is common and usually unnecessary. (3) We benchmark your spend against similar teams so you know if you're high or low.
